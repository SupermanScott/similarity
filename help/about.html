The Similarity Objects module is a tool for configuring similarity calculations against arbitrary Drupal entities. Its goal is to provide a central place for calculating similarities for other modules to use.

Properties of a Similarity Object

Conditions
Conditions provide means to filter the resulting objects allowing similarity calculations only to happen on objects that match the conditions against all other objects that match the same condition.
Tokens
Tokens are the actual pieces of information used for the similarity calculation. Common examples are the search index, taxonomy terms and votes. These tokens can then be transformed into other tokens.
